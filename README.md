#Overview
In this challenge we were cleaning raw data into a a concise dataframe for our clients. Our dataframe represented a the temperatures for the months of June and December in Hawaii ove a 6 year period. Creating a concise summary makes it easier for our clients to easily read and understand what was once a messy dataset.


#Results

Our results were that June had a had a higher mean tempurate than December. We were able to accomplish this distinction by using a dataframe to clean the data from the Hawaii.sqlite data sheet.
